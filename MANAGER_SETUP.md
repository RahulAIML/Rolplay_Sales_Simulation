# Sales AI - User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Sales AI Coach.

## Prerequisites
1.  **Outlook Account**: You must be the organizer of the meeting.
2.  **WhatsApp**: To receive coaching tips.

## Step 1: Register
1.  Go to the [Setup Page](https://rolplay-sales-simulation.onrender.com/setup).
2.  Enter your **Name**, **Outlook Email**, and **WhatsApp Number**.
3.  Click **Register**.
4.  You will receive a "Welcome" message on WhatsApp immediately.
    *   *If you don't receive it, check the number format and try again.*

## Step 2: Use the Coach
1.  Create a meeting in your Outlook Calendar.
1.  **Invite the Bot**: Add `coachlink360@outlook.com` as an attendee to your Outlook meeting.

## Step 3: Receive Coaching
*   **Before the meeting**: You will receive a WhatsApp message with a coaching plan (Scenario, Steps, Recommended Reply).
*   **After the meeting**: You will receive a reminder to log the outcome.
*   **Chat**: You can reply to the bot at any time for advice.
*   **Log**: Reply "Done" to mark the meeting complete and sync notes to HubSpot.

---
**Note**: You do NOT need to install any software or configure any integrations. Just invite the bot!
